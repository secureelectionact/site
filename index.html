The Need for the 28th Amendment

Dear U.S. Citizens, 

Due to election fraud by State and Local Government, I have written this Amendment. This Amendment is to Article 1-Section 4 of our U.S. Constitution. I have read our Constitution twice to e4nsure that anything in this Amendment does not interfere with anything other than Article 1 Section 4. I have spent 5 months carefully writing the Amendment.

This Amendment is going to be up to We the People to ensure that we have fair and honest elections to avoid fake installed politicians running our country.  

It is time for We the People to let our government know that they work for us! We do not work for them! 

Please Read the Amendment and share it nationwide with everyone. We must have this passed in order keep our country!
